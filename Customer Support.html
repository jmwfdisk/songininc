Frequently Asked Questions

What types of furniture do you manufacture?

We specialize in office and school furniture, including tables, chairs, desks, and storage solutions. Our products are designed for educational institutions, offices, and multi-purpose spaces.

Do you offer installation services?

Yes, we provide professional installation services for all our products. Our experienced team ensures proper setup and can handle projects of any size.

Can I customize the colors of your products?

Absolutely! We offer a wide range of color options for our seating and furniture. You can choose from various colors including Sky Blue, Red Violet, and many more to match your space.

What is your warranty policy?

We stand behind the quality of our products. Please contact us for specific warranty information based on the product you're interested in.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary depending on the product and order size. Standard orders typically take 2-4 weeks. For custom orders, please contact us for an estimated timeline.
